About

For over a decade I've filmed weddings and edited hundreds of hours of vows, speeches, and toasts — learning exactly where a story lives inside a long recording.

That experience became Corona Apps: a small independent studio building focused macOS tools for creators who work with real voices — filmmakers, podcasters, and anyone who needs to find the moment that matters.

Every app is designed around one belief: the story is already in the recording — you just need a faster way to cut it out.
