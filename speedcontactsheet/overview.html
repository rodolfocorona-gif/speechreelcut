What is Speed Contact Sheet?

Speed Contact Sheet is a macOS photo culling app built for photographers who shoot in volume — weddings, events, portraits, sports — and need to get from thousands of frames to a clean selection as quickly as possible. It is a dedicated culling tool, not an editor: its only job is to help you decide which photos to keep, reject or flag, and to do that faster than anything else in your workflow.

Every rating is written to a standard XMP sidecar file, so your selections flow straight into Adobe Lightroom, Capture One or Adobe Bridge. The app never moves or modifies your original photos.

Who is it for?

  • Wedding and event photographers facing 2,000–10,000 frames per job.
  • Portrait, fashion and product shooters who burst and need to pick the sharpest frame fast.
  • Anyone who finds Lightroom too slow for the first culling pass and wants a tool dedicated to selection.

Key features

Full-speed rating

Stars (1–5) and color labels with single-key shortcuts. Cull at the speed of thought.

Game controller support

Cull with an Xbox, PlayStation or MFi controller over Bluetooth or USB. Map navigation and ratings to buttons.

Non-destructive XMP

Ratings saved as standard XMP sidecars. Files are never moved or modified. Works with Lightroom, Capture One, Bridge.

Full RAW support

CR3, CR2, ARW, NEF, ORF, RW2, DNG and more — plus JPEG, HEIC, PNG, TIFF and automatic RAW+JPG pairing.

Built-in Pomodoro timer

A focus timer designed for long culling sessions, so a 10,000-photo job stays manageable.

Histogram & metadata

Live histogram and a full EXIF panel: camera, mode, white balance, shutter, aperture, ISO, lens and focal length.

Frequently asked questions

Is Speed Contact Sheet a good Photo Mechanic alternative?

Yes. It targets the same culling stage, keeps you in full control, and writes standard XMP metadata — while adding game-controller input and a built-in Pomodoro focus timer on macOS.

How is it different from Narrative Select?

Narrative Select uses AI to suggest selects. Speed Contact Sheet is fully human-driven: you make every call, and nothing is uploaded or analyzed by AI.

Will it change my original files?

No. Originals are never moved or modified. Ratings and color labels live in standard XMP sidecar files.

What formats are supported?

RAW (CR3, CR2, ARW, NEF, ORF, RW2, DNG and more), JPEG, HEIC, PNG and TIFF, with automatic RAW+JPG pairing.
