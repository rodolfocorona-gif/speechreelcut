Frequently asked

Are my files safe? Always. Speed Contact Sheet never moves or modifies your photos. Ratings and color labels are saved as standard XMP sidecar files compatible with professional photo workflows.

What formats are supported? RAW (CR3, CR2, ARW, NEF, ORF, RW2, DNG and more), JPEG, HEIC, PNG, and TIFF — with automatic RAW+JPG pairing.

Keyboard shortcuts? 1–5 stars, 0 clears, 6–9 colors, T/P tag, arrows navigate, Space preview, Z zoom, I info panel, Caps Lock auto-advance. Game controllers are also supported.
